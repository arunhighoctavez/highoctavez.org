High Octavez is an eclectic live music band founded by a group of IT professionals and Engineers with a vision to bring together people of different religions, languages and nations through the medium of light music. We perform music for all ages and genders, from recent and past.

We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All the profit from our shows are used in helping out medical needs of the people who are faced with a double challenge in life, that of an illness and the lack of money to treat the illness.
